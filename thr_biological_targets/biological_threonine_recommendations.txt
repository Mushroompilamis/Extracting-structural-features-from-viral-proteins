Targets are ranked at the sequence-residue level, not at the individual
symmetry-copy level. The score prioritizes buried interface contribution,
inter-chain/copy contacts, symmetry contacts, hydrogen-bond-like contacts,
partner diversity, and reasonable contact geometry. Low-burial symmetry-only
contacts are kept as secondary/peripheral candidates rather than automatically
selected as the best biological target.
